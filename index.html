FAQ

Questions We Get Asked

Is JapaPlanner really free to start? +
Yes, completely free. No credit card required. You can set up your profile, choose your destination country, see basic cost estimates, and start your Phase 1 checklist — all without paying anything.
How accurate are the cost estimates? +
Our costs are researched from official embassy websites, university fee pages, and real Japa stories from Nigerians who have relocated. We update the figures regularly and always include an exchange rate buffer to protect you from naira fluctuations.
Which countries are covered? +
Free plan covers UK. Premium plan covers UK, Canada, USA, Germany, and Australia — the top 5 Japa destinations for Nigerians.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. No penalties, no questions asked. Your data remains accessible on the free plan after cancellation.
Is my data safe? +
Yes. Your budget and savings data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties. You can delete your account and all data at any time.
